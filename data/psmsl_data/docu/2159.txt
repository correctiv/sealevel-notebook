=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 15-Jan-2013
Paphos is a new station supplied by the Oceanography Center of the University of Cyprus.
Measuring parameters: Pressure, Sea Temperature and Atmospheric Pressure.
Sensor Model: Paroscientific Digiquartz Intelligent sensor 8DP060-1.
Sensor Type: Pressure Type Sensor
Sampling rate : 15 seconds
Transmit Interval 1 minute.
