=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 12-Jul-2010
A three year International Polar Year project 'Longterm Sea Level Variability
in the Nordic Seas (LEVANS)' ran 2007-2009. This was funded by the 'Research
Council of Norway.
Project team: Vladimr Pavlov (Project co-ordination) from the Norwegian Polar
Institute and Igor Ashik from the Arctic and Antarctic Research Institute.
Data from 4 stations not previously held were forwarded to PSMSL
Bugrino 1952-1990
Mys Pikshueva 1955-1990
Polyarmy 1906-1990
Teriberka 1941-1990
