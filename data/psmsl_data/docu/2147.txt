=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 05-Sep-2012
Tel Aviv - Yaffo is a new station. Lat. 32 03'10"N Long. 34 44'59"E it is 
close to the site of the original Tel Aviv gauge. Data commences January 2011.
Primary benchmark is 55F 2.972m above gauge datum.
