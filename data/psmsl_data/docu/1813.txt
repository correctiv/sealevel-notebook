=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 18-Apr-2013
As part of the new REDMAR Spanish network a new MIROS radar gauge has been installed in Valencia from 2007 onwards. Datum differs by 0.969m this has been taken into account through the RLR factor. Previous factor 5.980m is now 6.949m.See paper B. Perez et al 'Overlapping sea level time series measured using different technologies: an example from the REDMAR Spanish network'.
