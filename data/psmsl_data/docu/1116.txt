=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 24-Aug-2010
Data supplier has confirmed that from August 1984, tide gauge zero is Chart Datum. Benchmark A112 is 10.136 m above Chart Datum. RLR (2008) is defined as 12.7 m below A112.
=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 14-Mar-2013
The reference datum changed from Chart Datum to Lowest Astronomical Tide (LAT) on 01/01/2008. The difference between the two is 0.26m the RLR factor has changed from 2.564m to 2.304m
