Zygi is a new station supplied by the Oceanography Center of the University of Cyprus.
Measuring Parameters: Pressure and Sea Temperature.
Sensor Model: AANDERRA Pressure sensor 3796A
Sensor Type: pressure type sensor
Sampling rate: 30 seconds.
Transmit Interval 1 minute.
