=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 23-Nov-2016
Tweed Entrance South is a new replacement station for Tweed Heads which has been closed for safety reasons it has not been levelled into the previous site. Using information supplied Tweed Entrance South has been made RLR. Primary benchmark is PM182920 3.087m above the Tweed River Hydro Datum.
