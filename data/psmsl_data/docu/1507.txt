ALL DUTCH METRIC DATA ARE MEASURED TO NAP (NORMAAL AMSTERDAMSCH PEIL)
=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 22-Jul-1991
LOCATED 900M TO THE WEST OF THE BROUWERSDAM. ALSO CALL "BG8".
=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 14-Aug-2001
Brouwershavense Gat built on 'pile' in sea, similar installation
to Haringvliet 10 (150/073).
=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 10-Feb-2011
Following information received concerning NAP both before and after the
re-levelling of 2005 most of the Dutch stations have been converted to RLR.
However this station is an offshore tide gauge which has not been re-visited so
remains 'Metric' at present.
