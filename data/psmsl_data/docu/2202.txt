=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 28-Jan-2014
Cristobal A is a new station. Complete dataset supplied by UHSLC. The historic data for three segments of this series i.e. historic Cristobal, Coco Solo and Limon Bay have been linked to a common network of benchmarks and the data have been calibrated with the use of tide staff readings. However areas of the data still look suspicious. Treat with caution.
