=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 23-Nov-2016
Shoal Bay is a replacement station for Port Stephens (Tomaree) as Tomaree Wharf was condemned it has not been levelled into the previous site. Using information supplied Shoal Bay has been made RLR. Primary benchmark is PM120624 4.422m above Port Stephens Hydro Datum.
