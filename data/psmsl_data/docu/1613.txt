=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 17-Oct-2005
There is a new John Hannah paper referring to New Zealand data for
Wellington, Dunedin, Lyttelton and Auckland. The reference is:
"An updated analysis of long-term sea level change in New Zealand."
Geophysical Research Letters, V31, L03307, doi:10.1029/2003GL019166
(2004).
=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 07-Aug-2013
The tide gauge at Gisborne is once again operational. LINZ have confirmed the datum remains the same.
=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 02-Sep-2014
Following the re-instatment of the tide gauge the supplying authority has been changed from Hydrographic Office, Royal New Zealand Navy to LINZ.
