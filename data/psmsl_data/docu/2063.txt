=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 24-Jan-2011
Alcudia is on the island of Majorca this is a new station first data 2009
which forms part of the newly established REDMAR network.  Gauge is a MIROS radar sensor.
Benchmark MAREOG-ALCUDIA is 2.307m relative to the REDMAR datum and 1.925m
relative to IGN. See paper B. Perez et al 'Overlapping sea level time series
measured using different technologies: an example from the REDMAR Spanish network'.
