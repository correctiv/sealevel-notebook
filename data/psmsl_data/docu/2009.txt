Erdemli is on the Eastern Mediterranean Coast. This station forms part of the
Turkish Sea Level Monitoring Network. Coast/Station code 310/071 id=2009. The
total dataset has been revised by the authority. This station was madev RLR.
RLR (2009) is 8.700m below TGBM ERDM-1. RLR factor is 5.827m.
