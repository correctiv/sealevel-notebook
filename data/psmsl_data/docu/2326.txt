Mayport (Bar Pilots Dock) Florida is a new NOAA station. This has been added to the database to give additional comparison stations.Using metadata from the NOAA website Mayport (Bar Pilots Dock), Florida has been made RLR from January 2001 onwards. Primary benchmark is 0218 A 1995 5.0m above the Station Datum.
