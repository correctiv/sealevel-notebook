=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 12-Jul-2010
Funchal II is a new station. Due to harbour construction the previous tide gauge was giving bad data results. The new gauge is levelled into a new TGBM and so cannot be added to the previous dataset.
=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 20-Jul-2010
This station has now been made RLR. RLR year is 2006 with RLR factor 5.421m. RLR (2006) is 9.5m below TGBM BH 20/02.
=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 06-Jun-2017
The PSMSL has been informed that there were major problems with the Funchal tide gauge (THALES) during 2015 with only monthly means for January being produced. The gauge was de-activated in mid July 2015 due to port works.
